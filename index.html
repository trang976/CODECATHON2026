Superfan Unlock

Backstage Pass: Stream Threshold → Exclusive Access

Every artist's tier below is fixed by their real play count in your history — Bronze, Silver, and Gold each unlock a different, more exclusive reward. There's nothing to slide or browse into early; you only ever see what you've actually earned.

How tiers work: Bronze (5,000 streams) unlocks a 10% merch code. Silver (15,000) unlocks access to an exclusive vinyl colorway. Gold (30,000) unlocks a single-use concert presale code. Tiers are cumulative — reaching Silver keeps your Bronze reward too — and each artist's tier is computed once from real data, not chosen by browsing.
Assumption we're making: these thresholds read like a cross-platform popularity count, not one person's personal plays — so we treat your own play count per artist as a personal superfan tier instead. Codes are deterministic (same input → same code) but connect to no real store; every "unlock" here is a mock UI state.
