Superfan Unlock

Stream Threshold → Exclusive Access

Cross a personal play-count threshold for an artist, and every one of that artist's albums in your history unlocks its own promo code — a mocked artist-collab tier, gated on real listening data.

5,000 plays
Assumption we're making: the brief's example threshold (30,000 streams) reads like a cross-platform popularity count, not one person's personal play count — no single listener here crosses that alone. We rebuilt it as a personal superfan tier instead: your own play count for an artist unlocks access, which an artist could offer through this platform. Promo codes are deterministic per album (real album data, not random each load) but not connected to any real store — every "unlock" here is a mock UI state, no real purchases happen.
